Application for Internship

An internship application letter is a formal request sent by students or fresh graduates to a company asking for an opportunity to work as an intern. Internships allow students to gain real industry experience, learn professional skills, and understand how companies operate. Many universities require internships as part of academic programs, while companies offer internships to identify talented students who may become future employees. A clear and professional internship application increases the chances of getting selected. Below are simple internship application formats in English and Hindi that can be copied and used when applying to companies or organizations.

English Application (Sample 1)

To, The HR Manager [Company Name] [Company Address] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Internship Respected Sir/Madam, I am writing to express my interest in applying for an internship opportunity at your organization. I am currently pursuing my [Course Name] from [College/University Name]. As part of my academic curriculum, I am required to complete an internship to gain practical knowledge and industry exposure. I am particularly interested in working in the field of [mention field such as marketing, finance, software development, etc.]. I believe that an internship at your organization would allow me to develop valuable professional skills and understand real workplace practices. I would be grateful if you could consider my application for an internship opportunity. Thank you for your time and consideration. Sincerely, Name: College / University: Course: Contact Number: Email:

English Application (Sample 2)

To, The Hiring Manager [Company Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Request for Internship Opportunity Dear Sir/Madam, I am a student of [course name] at [college/university name] and I am interested in gaining practical industry experience through an internship at your organization. Your company is well known for its professional environment and innovative work culture. I would be honored to gain learning experience under the guidance of your team. I assure you that I will work sincerely and make the most of this learning opportunity. Kindly consider my request for an internship position. Thank you. Regards, Name: Course: University: Phone: Email:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, एचआर प्रबंधक [कंपनी का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: इंटर्नशिप के लिए आवेदन महोदय / महोदया, सविनय निवेदन है कि मैं [कॉलेज/विश्वविद्यालय का नाम] में [कोर्स का नाम] का छात्र/छात्रा हूँ। अपने शैक्षणिक कार्यक्रम के अंतर्गत मुझे व्यावहारिक अनुभव प्राप्त करने के लिए इंटर्नशिप करना आवश्यक है। मैं [क्षेत्र का नाम – जैसे मार्केटिंग / सॉफ्टवेयर डेवलपमेंट / फाइनेंस] के क्षेत्र में सीखने और अनुभव प्राप्त करने के लिए उत्सुक हूँ। अतः आपसे विनम्र निवेदन है कि कृपया मुझे आपके प्रतिष्ठित संगठन में इंटर्नशिप करने का अवसर प्रदान करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। भवदीय, नाम: कॉलेज: कोर्स: संपर्क नंबर: ईमेल:

Tips for Writing an Internship Application

Keep the letter short and professional Mention your course and college clearly Explain why you want the internship Show interest in the company’s field Always include contact details

FAQ

Who can apply for internships? College students and recent graduates usually apply for internships. How long are internships? Most internships last from 4 weeks to 6 months. Do internships guarantee jobs? Not always, but they increase chances of getting hired. Is resume required? Yes, most companies ask for a resume along with the internship application.
