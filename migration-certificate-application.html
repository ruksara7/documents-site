Application for Migration Certificate

A migration certificate is an official document issued by a school, college, university, or education board. It confirms that a student has completed or left the institution and is allowed to take admission in another institution. Students usually need a migration certificate when they change schools, transfer to another college, move to a different university, or apply for higher studies. Below are simple **copy-ready migration certificate application formats in English and Hindi** for students.

Application for Migration Certificate (English)

To, The Principal [School / College Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Migration Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class/course ______ in your institution. I have completed my studies and now I wish to take admission in another school/college for further education. Therefore, I require a migration certificate. Kindly issue my migration certificate so that I can complete my admission process. I shall be grateful for your support. Thank you. Yours sincerely Name: Class / Course: Roll Number: Contact Number:

Documents Required

Application letter for migration certificate School or college ID card Fee clearance receipt Previous marksheet copy Transfer certificate (if required)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a migration certificate? A migration certificate is a document issued by a school, college, university, or board that confirms a student has left the institution and can join another institution. Why is migration certificate required? It is required when a student transfers to another school, college, or university. How long does it take to get a migration certificate? Most institutions issue migration certificates within 3 to 7 working days.
