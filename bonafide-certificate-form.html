FAQ

What is a bonafide certificate? A bonafide certificate is an official document issued by a school or college confirming that a person is currently a student of that institution. Why is bonafide certificate required? It is required for scholarship applications, hostel admission, bank account opening and government verification. Who issues the bonafide certificate? The certificate is issued by the school or college administration and signed by the principal.
