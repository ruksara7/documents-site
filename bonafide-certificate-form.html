FAQ

What is a bonafide certificate form? A bonafide certificate form is used by schools or colleges to confirm that a student is currently studying in that institution. Who issues the bonafide certificate? The certificate is issued by the school or college administration and signed by the principal. Why is a bonafide certificate required? It is required for scholarships, hostel admission, bank account opening and government verification.
