Bonafide Certificate Application

A bonafide certificate confirms that a student is currently studying in an institution. It is commonly required for scholarships, bank account opening, SIM verification, hostel admission, and government forms.

English Application

To, The Principal [School/College Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Bonafide Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class/course ______ in your institution. I require a bonafide certificate for [mention purpose – scholarship/bank/hostel/government form]. Kindly issue the certificate at the earliest. Thank you. Name: Class/Course: Roll No:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय/महोदया [विद्यालय/कॉलेज का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: बोनाफाइड प्रमाणपत्र हेतु आवेदन महोदय/महोदया, मैं आपके संस्थान की कक्षा/कोर्स ______ का छात्र/छात्रा हूँ। मुझे [उद्देश्य लिखें – छात्रवृत्ति/बैंक/होस्टल/सरकारी कार्य] हेतु बोनाफाइड प्रमाणपत्र की आवश्यकता है। कृपया प्रमाणपत्र जारी करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। नाम: कक्षा/कोर्स: रोल नंबर:

Common Purposes

Scholarship application Bank account opening SIM card verification Hostel admission Government form submission Internship verification

Documents Required

School ID card Fee receipt Aadhar card copy Application form

FAQ

How long does it take? Usually same day or 1–3 working days. Is fee required? Some institutions charge small fee. Can parents apply? Yes for minor students.
