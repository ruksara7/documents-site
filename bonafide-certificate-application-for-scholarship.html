FAQ

Why is bonafide certificate required for scholarship? Scholarship authorities require proof that the student is currently enrolled in a school or college. A bonafide certificate serves as official verification. Who issues the bonafide certificate? The certificate is issued by the school or college administration and signed by the principal or authorized authority. How long does it take to get a bonafide certificate? Most institutions issue it within the same day or within 1–3 working days. Is bonafide certificate mandatory for scholarship? Many government and private scholarships require it as proof of student status.
