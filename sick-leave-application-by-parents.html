Sick Leave Application by Parents

Parents write a sick leave application when a child cannot attend school due to illness such as fever, cold, infection, or medical treatment. Schools require written information for attendance records.

English Application

To, The Principal [School Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Sick Leave Application Respected Sir/Madam, My child [Student Name], studying in class ______, is suffering from illness and is unable to attend school. Kindly grant leave from ___ to ___. I assure you the student will attend school after recovery. Thank you. Parent Name: Signature: Contact Number:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय/महोदया [विद्यालय का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: बीमारी के कारण अवकाश हेतु आवेदन महोदय/महोदया, मेरा पुत्र/पुत्री [नाम] कक्षा ______ का छात्र/छात्रा है और बीमारी के कारण विद्यालय आने में असमर्थ है। कृपया दिनांक ___ से ___ तक अवकाश प्रदान करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। अभिभावक का नाम: मोबाइल नंबर:

Example Reasons

Fever Viral infection Doctor advised rest Food poisoning Eye infection

FAQ

Is medical certificate required? Sometimes for leave more than 3 days. Can WhatsApp message work? Some schools accept but written application preferred. Can parents sign? Yes, parents usually sign for minors.
