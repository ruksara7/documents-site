Leave Application for Marriage

Students sometimes need leave from school or college to attend a marriage in their family or relatives. Writing a proper leave application helps inform the school authority and request permission formally.

English Application

To, The Principal [School Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Leave Application for Marriage Respected Sir/Madam, I respectfully inform you that there is a marriage ceremony in my family on [date]. I need to attend this important family event. Therefore, I kindly request you to grant me leave for ___ day(s). I will complete my pending work after returning. Thank you. Yours obediently, Name: Class: Roll No:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय/महोदया [विद्यालय का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: विवाह समारोह के लिए अवकाश आवेदन महोदय/महोदया, सविनय निवेदन है कि मेरे परिवार में [तारीख] को विवाह समारोह है। मुझे इस कार्यक्रम में शामिल होना आवश्यक है। अतः कृपया मुझे ___ दिन का अवकाश प्रदान करने की कृपा करें। मैं लौटने के बाद अपनी पढ़ाई पूरी कर लूंगा/लूंगी। धन्यवाद। भवदीय, नाम: कक्षा: रोल नंबर:

Common Reasons

Brother's marriage Sister's marriage Cousin marriage Relative wedding ceremony Family wedding function

FAQ

How many days leave can be taken? Usually 1–5 days depending on school rules. Do we need proof? Some schools may ask for a wedding invitation. Can parents write this application? Yes, parents can write it for younger students.
