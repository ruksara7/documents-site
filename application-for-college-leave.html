Leave Application for College

College students may need leave due to illness, family work, or personal reasons. Use the application format below to request leave from your college authority.

English Application

To, The Principal [College Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Leave Application Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of [Course/Year] in your college. I request you to kindly grant me leave from ___ to ___ due to [reason]. I will attend the classes regularly after the leave period and complete the missed work. Kindly grant me leave for the above mentioned days. Thank you. Yours faithfully, Name: Course / Year: Roll Number:

Hindi Application
