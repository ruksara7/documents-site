Application for Scholarship

A scholarship application is written by students requesting financial support for education based on merit or financial need. It is commonly required for school and government scholarship programs.

English Application

To, The Principal / Scholarship Committee [School/College Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Scholarship Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class/course ______ in your institution. I have good academic performance but my family financial condition is weak. I kindly request you to grant me scholarship so that I can continue my education. Thank you. Name: Class/Course: Roll No:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य/छात्रवृत्ति समिति [विद्यालय/कॉलेज का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: छात्रवृत्ति हेतु आवेदन महोदय/महोदया, मैं आपके संस्थान की कक्षा/कोर्स ______ का छात्र/छात्रा हूँ। मेरी आर्थिक स्थिति कमजोर है। अतः कृपया मुझे छात्रवृत्ति प्रदान करने की कृपा करें ताकि मैं अपनी पढ़ाई जारी रख सकूँ। धन्यवाद। नाम: कक्षा/कोर्स: रोल नंबर:

Documents Required

Income certificate Previous marksheet Aadhar card copy Bank account details

FAQ

Who can apply? Students with merit or financial need. Is income certificate required? Yes in most cases. How long approval takes? Usually 15–30 days.
