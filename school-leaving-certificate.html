Application for School Leaving Certificate

English Student Format

To, The Principal [School Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for School Leaving Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I respectfully state that I am a student of your school. Due to personal reasons, I will not be able to continue my studies in this school. Kindly issue my School Leaving Certificate. Thank you. Yours faithfully, [Student Name] Class: Roll No:
