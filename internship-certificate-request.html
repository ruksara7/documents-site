Application for Internship Certificate

An internship certificate is an official document issued by a company or organization confirming that a student or trainee has successfully completed an internship. This certificate generally includes details such as the intern’s name, department, duration of internship, and nature of work performed. Students and fresh graduates often require this certificate for several purposes such as submitting proof of internship to their college, adding practical experience to their resume, applying for jobs, or applying for higher education programs. If the certificate is not issued automatically after completing the internship, the intern may need to submit a formal request letter to the HR department or supervisor asking for the internship completion certificate.

English Application

To, The HR Manager [Company/Organization Name] [Company Address] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Request for Internship Completion Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I hope you are doing well. I would like to inform you that I have successfully completed my internship at your esteemed organization in the [Department Name] from [Start Date] to [End Date]. During this internship period, I gained valuable practical knowledge and learned many professional skills that will help me in my academic and professional career. I kindly request you to issue my Internship Completion Certificate. This certificate is required for my academic records and future job applications. I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the organization and the entire team for giving me the opportunity to learn and work in a professional environment. I shall be grateful for your kind support. Thank you. Sincerely, Name: College / University: Course / Program: Contact Number: Email:

Common Reasons for Requesting Internship Certificate

Submitting internship proof to college or university Adding internship experience to resume or CV Applying for full-time jobs after graduation Applying for higher studies or professional programs Providing experience proof during job interviews

Documents That May Be Required

Internship offer letter or joining letter College ID card or student verification Internship completion report (if required by company) Personal identification document Email confirmation of internship completion

FAQ

Who issues the internship certificate? The HR department or supervisor of the company where the internship was completed. When should I request the certificate? Usually after completing the internship duration or during the final week of the internship. Is the internship certificate important? Yes. It serves as proof of practical work experience and is often required for job applications or academic records. Can the request be sent by email? Yes. Many interns request their internship certificate through a formal email to the HR department.
