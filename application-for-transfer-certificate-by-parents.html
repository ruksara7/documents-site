Application for Transfer Certificate by Parents

A transfer certificate (TC) is required when a student changes school due to relocation, admission to another school, or personal reasons. Parents write this request to obtain the TC and marksheet after clearing dues. Schools normally issue the transfer certificate within 2 to 7 working days after verification and fee clearance.

English Parent Application

To, The Principal [School Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Transfer Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I am the parent of [Student Name], studying in class ______ of your school. Due to [mention reason — shifting to another city / admission in another school / personal reasons], we are unable to continue studies in your institution. Therefore, I kindly request you to issue the Transfer Certificate and latest marksheet. All school dues have been cleared. Thank you. Sincerely, Parent/Guardian Name: Mobile Number: Student Name: Class: Roll No:

Hindi Parent Application

FAQ

Who can request TC? Parent or legal guardian. Can school refuse TC? Only if dues are pending. How long does it take? Usually 2–7 working days. Is marksheet given with TC? Yes, most schools provide latest marksheet.
