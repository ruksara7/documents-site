Application for Library Card for Students

This page provides a simple application for library card for school or college students. Students can copy the English or Hindi format and submit it to the librarian or principal to request a new library card.

Application for Library Card in English

To, The Librarian [School / College Name] [Address] Subject: Application for Issuing Library Card Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class [Class/Department] in your institution. I kindly request you to issue me a library card so that I can access the library facilities and borrow books for my studies. I assure you that I will follow all the library rules and regulations and take proper care of the books issued to me. Kindly issue my library card at the earliest. Thank you. Yours faithfully, [Student Name] [Class / Roll Number] [Section] [Contact Number] [Date]

लाइब्रेरी कार्ड के लिए आवेदन (Hindi Format)

सेवा में, पुस्तकालयाध्यक्ष [विद्यालय / महाविद्यालय का नाम] [पता] विषय: लाइब्रेरी कार्ड जारी करने हेतु आवेदन महोदय/महोदया, सविनय निवेदन है कि मैं आपके विद्यालय/महाविद्यालय का छात्र/छात्रा हूँ तथा कक्षा [Class] में अध्ययन कर रहा/रही हूँ। कृपया मुझे लाइब्रेरी कार्ड जारी करने की कृपा करें ताकि मैं पुस्तकालय की सुविधाओं का उपयोग कर सकूँ। मैं आपको आश्वासन देता/देती हूँ कि पुस्तकालय के सभी नियमों का पालन करूँगा/करूँगी तथा पुस्तकों का सही ढंग से उपयोग करूँगा/करूँगी। अतः आपसे निवेदन है कि कृपया मुझे लाइब्रेरी कार्ड जारी करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। भवदीय, [छात्र का नाम] [कक्षा / रोल नंबर] [सेक्शन] [मोबाइल नंबर] [दिनांक]

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I write an application for library card?

You should write the application to the librarian or principal requesting the issue of a library card so you can access library books.

2. Who should submit a library card application?

Students who want to borrow books from the school or college library usually submit this application.

3. Can I copy this application format?

Yes, you can copy the format above and submit it in your school or college.
