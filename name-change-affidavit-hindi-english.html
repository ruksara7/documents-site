Name Change Affidavit

A name change affidavit is a sworn declaration stating that a person has changed their name and both the old and new names refer to the same person. It is commonly used for passport applications, bank records, educational documents, and government records.

Hindi Name Change Affidavit

शपथ पत्र मैं ______ पुत्र/पुत्री ______ निवासी ______ यह शपथपूर्वक घोषणा करता/करती हूँ कि मेरा पूर्व नाम ______ था और अब मैंने अपना नाम बदलकर ______ कर लिया है। आगे से सभी सरकारी एवं निजी अभिलेखों में मेरा नाम ______ ही प्रयोग किया जाएगा। मैं यह घोषणा करता/करती हूँ कि उपरोक्त दोनों नाम एक ही व्यक्ति के हैं। यह शपथ पत्र मेरे द्वारा पूर्ण होशोहवास में दिया जा रहा है। दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ शपथकर्ता के हस्ताक्षर नाम: ______ नोटरी द्वारा सत्यापित

English Name Change Affidavit

AFFIDAVIT I, ______ son/daughter of ______ resident of ______ do hereby solemnly affirm and declare that my former name was ______ and I have changed my name to ______. Hereafter, I shall be known by the name ______ for all purposes including official records. Both the names refer to the same person. This affidavit is made to declare the change of my name. Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Signature of Deponent Name: ______ Verified before Notary Public
