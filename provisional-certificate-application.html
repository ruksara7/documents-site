Application for Migration Certificate

Students need a migration certificate when transferring from one school, college, or university to another. Below are simple **copy-ready migration certificate applications in English and Hindi**.

English Application

To The Principal [School / College Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Migration Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class/course ______ in your institution. I have completed my studies and wish to take admission in another institution for further education. Therefore, I require a migration certificate. Kindly issue my migration certificate so that I can complete my admission process. Thank you. Yours sincerely Name: Class / Course: Roll Number: Contact Number:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय / महोदया [विद्यालय / कॉलेज का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: माइग्रेशन प्रमाणपत्र हेतु आवेदन महोदय / महोदया, मैं आपके संस्थान की कक्षा / कोर्स ______ का छात्र / छात्रा हूँ। मैं आगे की पढ़ाई के लिए किसी अन्य विद्यालय / कॉलेज में प्रवेश लेना चाहता / चाहती हूँ। इसलिए मुझे माइग्रेशन प्रमाणपत्र की आवश्यकता है। कृपया मुझे माइग्रेशन प्रमाणपत्र जारी करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। भवदीय नाम: कक्षा / कोर्स: रोल नंबर: मोबाइल नंबर:

Quick Tips

  • Write the correct school or college name.
  • Mention your class or course clearly.
  • Add your contact number.
  • Keep the application polite and simple.

Common Purposes

  • College admission in another institution
  • University transfer
  • Board change
  • Higher education admission

Documents Required

  • Application letter
  • School or college ID card
  • Fee clearance receipt
  • Previous marksheet copy

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a migration certificate? A document that confirms a student has left one institution and can join another.
  • Who issues migration certificate? School, college, university, or examination board.
  • How long does it take? Usually 3–7 working days.
